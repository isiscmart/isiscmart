GRANDMA'S LENTIL SOUP | 60 minutes

INGREDIENTS

  • Dried Lentils - 1 cup
  • Vegetable broth - 4 cups
  • 1 Onion - diced
  • 2 Carrots - diced
  • 2 Celery stalks - diced


INSTRUCTIONS

STEP ONE

Let's get started on this delicious journey! Begin by giving those lentils a good rinse under cold water, just like Grandma always did. Drain them well and set aside.


STEP TWO

Next up, heat a drizzle of olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Toss in the diced onion, carrots, and celery, and let them work their magic until they're soft and fragrant, about 5 minutes or so.


STEP THREE

Now, it's time to add in those lovely lentils and pour in the veggie broth. Bring it all to a gentle boil, then lower the heat and let it simmer away, filling your kitchen with the most amazing aromaâ€”just like Grandma's kitchen used to smell. Let it simmer for about 20-25 minutes, until those lentils are tender and oh-so-delicious.


STEP FOUR

Season your soup with a pinch of salt and pepper to taste, and then it's time to ladle out some love. Serve up this heartwarming soup in your favorite bowls, and get ready to cozy up with each and every spoon.
